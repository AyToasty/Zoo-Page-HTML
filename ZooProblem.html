Bears

  • Ollie

    • The grizzly bear which is a subspecies of the brown bears inhabiting North America. Standing as tall as 8 feet and weighing up to 800 pounds. They are highly intelligent and fast, reaching speeds up to 35 miles per hour.

  • Mona

    • The polar bear is a close relative of the brown bear but resides in the Artic. One of the largest land based predators. Polar bears can grow up to 8 feet long and weighing up to 1700 pounds. They have large paws to help them navigate through the ice.
